What We Do

Connect Students with resources

It can be overwhelming to navigate the array of resources that the CS department and Brown in general offer for students in need of support. We help students figure out what to do.

Communicate Student Concerns

During monthly diversity commitee meetings, advocates share students concerns, priorities, and suggestions with the leaders of the CS department.
